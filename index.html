Stantosonic Milestones

07 April 2023 - Irrs:inn

Irrs:inn by Max Nogo was the first release with German lyrics on Stantosonic. A desperate but humorous plea to balance respect for all genders and the German language.

06 May 2022 - YA YA

YA YA, Klawumation's first release on Stantosonic soon became the Stantosonic's most successful release to date. According to Spotify statistics it generated more streams than any other Stantosonic release and reached more than 60 countries around the world.

01 April 2022 - HELLO TO THE STARS

Matthias Maurer's space mission "Cosmic Kiss" inspired his school mate's tribute to the heroes of funk. That's how it became the first solo release of the label founder.

17 Dec 2021 - NO MEANS NO

Dacite's first release and the first release ever on Stantosonic. The rock-pop role model that laid the foundation.

About Stantosonic

Over the years, stantosonic has become home to a diverse family of innovative artists. While sound takes center stage, visuals make our releases complete.

If you feel our support is just what you need to make your ideas fly, don’t hesitate to contact us. If you feel inspired and would like to bring you’re creativity, motivation or talent to the team: we’re open for collaborations - especially singers and instrumentalists are welcome to bring even more diversity to our projects.

If you feel like more and would like to help us give you more of our output: please give us your thoughts or support.
