About Stantosonic

Over the years, stantosonic has become home to a diverse family of innovative artists. While sound takes center stage, visuals make our releases complete.

If you feel our support is just what you need to make your ideas fly, don’t hesitate to contact us. If you feel inspired and would like to bring you’re creativity, motivation or talent to the team: we’re open for collaborations - especially singers and instrumentalists are welcome to bring even more diversity to our projects.

If you feel like more and would like to help us give you more of our output: please give us your thoughts or support.
